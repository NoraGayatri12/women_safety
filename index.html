Welcome to Women Safety Portal

This portal is dedicated to empowering women with knowledge, resources, and tools to stay safe and confident in their everyday lives.

Emergency Contacts

Save these numbers in your phone and share with friends and family. In any emergency, don’t hesitate to reach out immediately.

General Safety Tips

  • Stay aware of your surroundings at all times.
  • Trust your instincts; if something feels wrong, leave immediately.
  • Keep your phone charged and with you.
  • Inform a trusted person about your whereabouts.

Self-Defense Tips

  • Use elbows and knees—they are powerful and effective.
  • Aim for vulnerable areas: eyes, nose, throat, and groin.
  • Practice yelling loudly to attract attention.
  • Enroll in local self-defense classes for hands-on training.

Violence Against Women

Violence against women can take many forms, and it is important to recognize and address it. Awareness and support can help prevent and reduce such incidents.

If you or someone you know is experiencing violence, reach out for help. You're not alone.

Safe Travel Tips

  • Use trusted transportation apps and services.
  • Avoid isolated or poorly lit areas, especially at night.
  • Share your live location with a trusted contact.
  • Carry personal safety items like pepper spray or alarms.
